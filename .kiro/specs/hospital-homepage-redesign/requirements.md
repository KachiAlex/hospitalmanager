# Requirements Document

## Introduction

This specification defines the requirements for redesigning the T-Happy Home Hospital homepage to match the professional, modern design style of Awesome Grace Hospital. The redesign will transform the current basic homepage into a sophisticated medical website that builds trust and credibility with patients while maintaining all functional requirements.

## Glossary

- **T-Happy_System**: The complete hospital management web application
- **Homepage**: The main landing page that users see when visiting the website
- **Hero_Section**: The prominent top section with main messaging and call-to-action
- **Medical_Branding**: Professional color scheme and visual identity appropriate for healthcare
- **Patient_Journey**: The flow of information and actions a patient takes on the website
- **Responsive_Design**: Layout that adapts to different screen sizes and devices
- **Professional_Imagery**: High-quality photos of medical facilities, staff, and patients
- **Trust_Indicators**: Elements that build credibility (testimonials, certifications, contact info)

## Requirements

### Requirement 1: Modern Hero Section Design

**User Story:** As a potential patient, I want to see a professional, welcoming hero section that immediately communicates the hospital's expertise and care quality, so that I feel confident in choosing this healthcare provider.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Hero_Section SHALL display "Expert Care in Neurosurgery" as the primary headline
2. WHEN the page loads, THE Hero_Section SHALL show a high-quality image of a healthcare professional with a patient
3. THE Hero_Section SHALL include a prominent "Book an Appointment" call-to-action button
4. THE Hero_Section SHALL use a professional blue gradient background consistent with medical branding
5. THE Hero_Section SHALL include supporting text about the hospital's compassionate, personal touch approach
6. THE Hero_Section SHALL maintain visual hierarchy with proper typography sizing and spacing

### Requirement 2: Professional About Us Section

**User Story:** As a patient researching healthcare options, I want to see information about the hospital's specializations and approach, so that I can understand their expertise and patient-centered care philosophy.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE About_Section SHALL display "Specialized Experts Dedicated to Saving Lives" as the main heading
2. WHEN displaying hospital information, THE About_Section SHALL include a professional photo of the hospital interior or reception area
3. THE About_Section SHALL highlight the hospital's areas of expertise: Neurosurgery, Brain & Spine, Dialysis, and ICU Services
4. THE About_Section SHALL include text about patient-centric approach and specialized care
5. THE About_Section SHALL feature a "Learn More" call-to-action button
6. THE About_Section SHALL use consistent typography and spacing with the overall design

### Requirement 3: Enhanced Services Section with Real Medical Imagery

**User Story:** As a patient, I want to see the hospital's services with actual photos of medical facilities and equipment, so that I can understand the quality and scope of care available.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Services_Section SHALL display "Prompt, Profound & Professional Medical Care Tailored to Your Needs" as the section heading
2. WHEN showing services, THE Services_Section SHALL include high-quality photos of actual medical facilities for each service
3. THE Services_Section SHALL feature three primary services: Neurosurgery, Brain & Spine Treatment, and Dialysis
4. WHEN displaying Neurosurgery service, THE Services_Section SHALL show an operating room or surgical facility image
5. WHEN displaying Brain & Spine service, THE Services_Section SHALL show relevant medical equipment or treatment room
6. WHEN displaying Dialysis service, THE Services_Section SHALL show dialysis treatment facility with proper equipment
7. THE Services_Section SHALL include detailed descriptions for each service highlighting expertise and patient care

### Requirement 4: Patient-Centric Why Choose Us Section

**User Story:** As someone considering healthcare options, I want to understand what makes this hospital different and better, so that I can make an informed decision about my care.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Why_Choose_Section SHALL display "Dedicated to Saving Lives" as the main heading
2. THE Why_Choose_Section SHALL include "Our Approach is Patient-Centric" as a subheading
3. WHEN listing hospital advantages, THE Why_Choose_Section SHALL include "24/7 Specialized Medical Care"
4. THE Why_Choose_Section SHALL feature "World-Class Facilities & Technology" as a key benefit
5. THE Why_Choose_Section SHALL highlight "Personalized Patient Experience" as a core value
6. THE Why_Choose_Section SHALL mention "Experienced, Compassionate Experts" as a differentiator
7. THE Why_Choose_Section SHALL include a professional photo of the hospital building exterior
8. THE Why_Choose_Section SHALL feature a "Get In Touch With Us" call-to-action button

### Requirement 5: Professional Appointment Booking Section

**User Story:** As a patient, I want a clear and professional way to book appointments, so that I can easily schedule my healthcare needs without confusion.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Booking_Section SHALL display "Book an Appointment with Us!" as the main heading
2. THE Booking_Section SHALL include explanatory text about quick treatments and no long waiting times
3. THE Booking_Section SHALL feature "Simply reserve a time slot, walk in at the appointed time and get prioritized treatments at no extra cost" as supporting information
4. THE Booking_Section SHALL include a prominent "Reserve My Slot" call-to-action button
5. THE Booking_Section SHALL use a professional blue background consistent with medical branding
6. THE Booking_Section SHALL include a relevant medical facility or consultation image

### Requirement 6: Patient Testimonials Section

**User Story:** As a potential patient, I want to read testimonials from other patients, so that I can understand the quality of care and patient satisfaction at this hospital.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Testimonials_Section SHALL display "What Our Clients Say" as the section heading
2. THE Testimonials_Section SHALL include at least three patient testimonials
3. WHEN displaying testimonials, THE Testimonials_Section SHALL include patient names and photos
4. THE Testimonials_Section SHALL feature testimonials that highlight different aspects of care (recovery, staff quality, facilities)
5. THE Testimonials_Section SHALL use a clean, professional layout with proper spacing
6. THE Testimonials_Section SHALL maintain patient privacy while showing authentic feedback

### Requirement 7: Newsletter Signup and Health Tips

**User Story:** As someone interested in health information, I want to sign up for health tips and updates, so that I can stay informed about healthcare topics and hospital news.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Newsletter_Section SHALL display "SIGN UP FOR OUR HEALTH TIPS" as the main heading
2. THE Newsletter_Section SHALL include "Get notified everytime we publish a health blog post" as supporting text
3. THE Newsletter_Section SHALL provide an email input field for subscription
4. THE Newsletter_Section SHALL include a "Subscribe Now" call-to-action button
5. THE Newsletter_Section SHALL use a red background consistent with medical emergency branding
6. THE Newsletter_Section SHALL include privacy policy and terms of service acknowledgment text

### Requirement 8: Enhanced Contact and Location Information

**User Story:** As a patient, I want clear contact information and location details, so that I can easily reach the hospital or visit in person.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Contact_Section SHALL display prominent "Call Today" information with phone numbers
2. THE Contact_Section SHALL include "Visit Us" section with complete address information
3. WHEN showing location, THE Contact_Section SHALL display "Head office: 33, Ishaga Road, Surulere Lagos"
4. THE Contact_Section SHALL include "Branch office: 6, Ogunlana Drive, Surulere, Lagos" information
5. THE Contact_Section SHALL feature a "CONTACT US" call-to-action button
6. THE Contact_Section SHALL use professional blue background consistent with branding
7. THE Contact_Section SHALL include WhatsApp contact integration for modern communication

### Requirement 9: Professional Medical Branding and Color Scheme

**User Story:** As a website visitor, I want to see consistent, professional medical branding throughout the site, so that I perceive the hospital as trustworthy and credible.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Medical_Branding SHALL use a primary blue color scheme (#4338ca to #6366f1 gradient range)
2. THE Medical_Branding SHALL use red accents (#dc2626 to #ef4444) for emergency and call-to-action elements
3. THE Medical_Branding SHALL maintain consistent typography using professional fonts (Inter, Roboto)
4. THE Medical_Branding SHALL ensure proper contrast ratios for accessibility compliance
5. THE Medical_Branding SHALL use white and light gray backgrounds for clean, medical appearance
6. THE Medical_Branding SHALL apply consistent spacing and layout principles throughout all sections

### Requirement 10: Responsive Design and Mobile Optimization

**User Story:** As a user accessing the website on various devices, I want the redesigned homepage to work perfectly on mobile, tablet, and desktop, so that I can access hospital information regardless of my device.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Responsive_Design SHALL adapt all sections to mobile, tablet, and desktop screen sizes
2. WHEN viewed on mobile devices, THE Responsive_Design SHALL maintain readability and usability
3. THE Responsive_Design SHALL ensure touch targets are appropriately sized for mobile interaction
4. THE Responsive_Design SHALL optimize images for different screen densities and sizes
5. THE Responsive_Design SHALL maintain visual hierarchy and content priority across all breakpoints
6. THE Responsive_Design SHALL ensure fast loading times on all devices

### Requirement 11: Professional Photography Integration

**User Story:** As a potential patient, I want to see real photos of the hospital facilities, staff, and equipment, so that I can visualize the quality and professionalism of the healthcare environment.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Professional_Imagery SHALL include high-quality photos of actual medical facilities
2. THE Professional_Imagery SHALL feature images of operating rooms, consultation areas, and medical equipment
3. THE Professional_Imagery SHALL include photos of healthcare professionals in appropriate medical settings
4. THE Professional_Imagery SHALL show patient care scenarios while maintaining privacy and dignity
5. THE Professional_Imagery SHALL use consistent lighting, color grading, and professional photography standards
6. THE Professional_Imagery SHALL optimize all images for web performance while maintaining quality

### Requirement 12: Trust and Credibility Elements

**User Story:** As someone choosing a healthcare provider, I want to see elements that demonstrate the hospital's credibility and trustworthiness, so that I feel confident in their medical expertise.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Trust_Elements SHALL include patient testimonials with authentic names and experiences
2. THE Trust_Elements SHALL display professional certifications or accreditations where applicable
3. THE Trust_Elements SHALL feature clear contact information and multiple communication channels
4. THE Trust_Elements SHALL include detailed service descriptions that demonstrate medical expertise
5. THE Trust_Elements SHALL maintain professional language and medical terminology accuracy
6. THE Trust_Elements SHALL provide transparent information about services, locations, and contact methods
